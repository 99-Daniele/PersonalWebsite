Master Thesis Projects
Sep 2023 - Jul 2024

My master's thesis project, titled "Analysis of the Trials of the Civil Court of Milan," involved a detailed data analysis of the trials at the Civil Court of Milan. The goal was to identify the causes of the high duration of trials.

The data consisted of a database containing all recorded juridical events, which I cleaned, aggregated, and transformed into useful information about the structure and duration of trials. I then created a software tool from scratch, implemented in Python, that allows users to interact with, filter, and visualize graphs.

Moreover, since most of the analyzed trials were still ongoing, I implemented a regression model to predict the final duration of these trials. Finally, I analyzed the results to identify the critical issues and problems within the civil trial system.

Java / UML / Testing / Teamwork / Project Management
